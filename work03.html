Just Breathe

GitHub Repo

Just Breathe is a meditation aid in which you would push the spacebar with every breath. It helps you track your longest and shortst breath, as well as an average so you may track your progress

Technologies Used:

Framework: Sinatra

Frontend: CSS

JS Libraries: MousetrapJS, UnderscoreJS
