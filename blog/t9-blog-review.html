Website Rehauling

Hello agian. I wanted to quickly go over a recent thing I did for my site. I went through and revamped the look, I hope you like it! What lead me to do this was the feedback I asked for and recieved.

I recieved feedback about colors, fonts, sizes and various other items which I put into consideration in making my new site. I can definitely admit to learnings certain things along the process with regards to my approach:

1. You begin to obsess over the design. You sometimes have to just let something be because you will eventually go crazy. A site is an evolution and it can always be tweaked.
2. I favor simple layouts with minimalistic colors. I tried initially to do too much on the page and it became flooded.
3. Fonts mean a lot. What I mean is that choosing fonts can make or break the look. I also like to put in different fonts to break the visual up a bit and not make the page look bland.

I'm sure I will look back at this update and see how far I have come from this point, but I can certainly admit to learning a ton up to now! Thanks for reading
